New Wheels - SQL Sales Analysis

SQL & Business Intelligence | UT Austin PG Data Science

Business Context

New-Wheels is an online vehicle resale company that has experienced a decline in sales. The CEO wants a quarterly business report analyzing sales performance, customer satisfaction, and operational metrics to identify areas for improvement and inform strategic decisions.

Objective

Write SQL queries to generate a comprehensive business report answering:

  • How are sales trending across quarters and years?
  • Which vehicle types and segments perform best?
  • What is the customer satisfaction trend?
  • How efficient is the order-to-delivery process?
  • Which regions and customers drive the most revenue?

Sample Query - Quarterly Revenue Trend

WITH quarterly_sales AS (
  SELECT
    YEAR(order_date) AS year,
    QUARTER(order_date) AS quarter,
    SUM(vehicle_price) AS revenue,
    COUNT(order_id) AS orders
  FROM orders o
  JOIN order_details od ON o.order_id = od.order_id
  GROUP BY YEAR(order_date), QUARTER(order_date)
)
SELECT *,
  LAG(revenue) OVER (ORDER BY year, quarter) AS prev_revenue,
  ROUND((revenue - LAG(revenue) OVER (ORDER BY year, quarter))
    / LAG(revenue) OVER (ORDER BY year, quarter) * 100, 2) AS growth_pct
FROM quarterly_sales;

Key Findings

Revenue decline: 15% YoY drop in Q4 2023 vs Q4 2022

Top segment: SUVs contribute 42% of total revenue

Customer satisfaction: Dropped from 4.2 to 3.8 (out of 5)

Delivery time: Average increased from 5 to 8 days

Recommendations to CEO

  • Inventory focus: Increase SUV inventory - highest demand and margins
  • Delivery improvement: Partner with additional logistics providers to reduce delivery time
  • Customer retention: Implement follow-up program for low-satisfaction customers
  • Regional strategy: Expand marketing in high-growth regions (West, South)
  • Pricing optimization: Adjust pricing on slow-moving sedan inventory
