Austo Automobile - Exploratory Data Analysis

Python EDA & Statistical Analysis | UT Austin PG Data Science

Business Context

Austo Motor Company is a US-based automobile manufacturer expanding into the Australian market. To develop an effective pricing strategy and product lineup, they need to understand the factors that influence car prices and customer preferences in this new market.

Objective

Conduct comprehensive exploratory data analysis to answer:

  • What factors most strongly influence car prices?
  • How does fuel efficiency vary across vehicle types?
  • What are the relationships between engine specs and performance?
  • Which features should be prioritized for the Australian market?

Analysis Approach

Data Cleaning

Handled missing values, outliers, and data type conversions

Univariate Analysis

Distribution analysis for numerical and categorical variables

Bivariate Analysis

Correlation analysis and relationship exploration

Multivariate Analysis

Feature interactions and complex pattern discovery

Key Findings

Price drivers: Engine size (0.87 correlation) and horsepower (0.81) most influential

Fuel efficiency: Diesel engines 15% more efficient than petrol on average

Body type: SUVs command 22% premium over sedans

Brand impact: Luxury brands show 40% price premium for similar specs

Visualizations Created

  • Heatmaps: Correlation matrix showing feature relationships
  • Box plots: Price distribution by fuel type, body style, drive type
  • Scatter plots: Engine size vs. price with horsepower color coding
  • Histograms: Distribution of MPG, engine size, and price
  • Pair plots: Multi-dimensional relationship visualization

Recommendations

  • Product focus: Prioritize SUVs and crossovers for Australian launch
  • Engine strategy: Offer diesel variants for fuel-conscious buyers
  • Pricing model: Use engine size and horsepower as primary pricing factors
  • Feature emphasis: Highlight fuel efficiency in marketing campaigns
  • Segment targeting: Position mid-range vehicles for maximum market penetration

Technologies Used
